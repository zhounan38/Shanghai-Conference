About the Conference

Welcome to the academic conference on "Firm Strategy in the Age of AI" at Tongji University. This event will bring together experts from academia to discuss the impact of artificial intelligence on business strategies. Our conference will delve into the latest research, trends, and practical applications of AI in business strategy. Join us for two days of insightful presentations, engaging discussions, and networking opportunities with thought leaders and peers. We look forward to welcoming you to Tongji University and fostering meaningful discussions on the future of AI in business strategy.

Conference Topics

  • AI and Business Strategy
  • AI in Multinational Management
  • AI and Innovation
  • AI and Platforms
  • Ethics of AI in Business
  • Future Trends in AI
